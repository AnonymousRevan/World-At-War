[h1]A World at War[/h1]

[b]Compatible with version 1.14.2
   Compatible with all DLCs[/b]

[h1]Overview:[/h1]

A World at War is a multiplayer historical mod with the primary goal of providing a balanced and enjoyable experience. Taking aspects of Hearts of Oak, World Ablaze, and Their Finest Bruh, the mod aims to expand upon the successes of other mods to create a unique blend of mechanics and game styles. 

General Features:

- Playability improvements focus on the player's experience against other players to provide great gameplay within the rich history of the Second World War.

- Includes technology trees for the 4 major factions that are expanded from Vanilla. 

- Expanded political advisors window for economic, research, and combat benefits.

- Industry changes include: larger factory counts, extra building options, larger state capacity, but CIV and MIL IC is reduced.

[h1]Military changes:[/h1]
[list]
[*]All unit battalions are 2 combat width
[*]All terrain is 90 combat width and 60 reinforcement width (with the exception of Urban terrain being 120 combat width)
[*]Unit and equipment stats all heavily adjusted
[/list]
